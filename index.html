About the Republican Party

The Grand Old Party (GOP)

The Republican Party was founded in 1854 initially in order to combat slavery. Now, it is one of the 2 major political parties, committed to upholding American traditional values that have existed since the days of the Founding Fathers. We fight for a strong and free United States that will allow everyone to chase the American Dream.
